Privacy Policy

Signal Breach — Nexvolt Studios
Last updated: March 18, 2026

Nexvolt Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Signal Breach mobile application (the "Game"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game.

Information We Collect

Signal Breach does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the Game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — We use AdMob to display advertisements in the Game. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and general location data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Games Services — If you sign in with your Google account, we use Google Play Games Services for leaderboard functionality. This may collect your Google Play display name and game scores. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Signal Breach displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to provide ads relevant to you. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases (such as avatar frames, character customization items, and ad removal). These transactions are processed entirely through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Storage

Game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores may be stored on Google Play Games servers if you choose to sign in. We do not operate our own servers for data storage.

Children's Privacy

Signal Breach is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our Game, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the Game
  • Request deletion of your leaderboard data by contacting us

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
